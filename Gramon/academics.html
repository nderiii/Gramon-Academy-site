Our Academic Structure

Pre-Primary 1 & 2 (Ages 4-6)

Our Early Years program provides a nurturing environment where young learners develop foundational skills through play-based learning and discovery.

  • Language and literacy development
  • Numeracy and basic math concepts
  • Social-emotional learning
  • Biblical stories and values
  • Creative arts and music

Grades 1-3 (Ages 6-9)

Lower Primary builds core competencies while fostering curiosity and a love for learning from a Christian perspective.

  • Literacy and communication skills
  • Mathematical thinking and problem-solving
  • Environmental activities
  • Christian religious education
  • Creative arts and physical education

Grades 4-6 (Coming Soon)

Our upcoming Upper Primary program will continue developing competencies with increased academic rigor and spiritual growth opportunities.

  • Advanced literacy and composition
  • STEM subjects with Christian worldview
  • Social studies and citizenship
  • Bible study and Christian leadership
  • Career and life skills

Key Features of Our Program

Learner-Centered

Our approach recognizes each child's unique abilities and learning style, tailoring instruction accordingly.

Inquiry-Based

Students develop critical thinking through exploration, questioning, and discovery.

Collaborative

Teamwork and communication skills are developed through group projects and activities.

Faith-Integrated

Biblical principles are naturally incorporated into all subject areas.

Technology-Enhanced

Digital tools are used purposefully to enhance learning experiences.

Growth-Focused

Assessment emphasizes progress and mastery rather than just grading.

Our Assessment Approach

At Gramon Christian School, we believe assessment should measure growth and guide instruction rather than simply assign grades.

1

Continuous Assessment

Ongoing evaluation through observations, projects, and activities

2

Competency-Based

Focus on demonstrating mastery of skills and knowledge

3

Holistic Evaluation

Assessing academic, spiritual, and character development
