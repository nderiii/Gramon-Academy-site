Parents & Community

PARTNERING WITH FAMILIES

We believe parents are the primary educators, and we are honored to walk alongside families in their children's education. Gramon Christian School maintains open, collaborative communication with parents through regular updates, school events, and parenting forums.

We encourage parents' involvement through:

  • PTA (Parent-Teacher Association)
  • Regular one-on-one parent and teacher engagement
  • School events and functions

ROOTED IN CHRIST

At Gramon, faith is the foundation of all we do. Every lesson, every activity, every interaction is guided by biblical truth and the love of Jesus. Through weekly chapel services, daily devotions, and spiritual mentorship, we help learners develop a personal relationship with God and a strong moral compass.

We believe in shaping hearts before shaping minds—preparing students to be ethical, compassionate, and faith-driven leaders.
