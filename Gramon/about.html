About Gramon Christian School

Where CBC meets Christian Values to nurture learners in character, faith, and excellence.

To provide a Christ-centered, CBC-aligned education that nurtures every child's potential and prepares them to thrive in life and eternity.

Our Story

Founded with a passion for excellence and a calling to serve, Gramon Christian School was established to meet the growing need for a values-driven, skills-based education in Kenya.

We combine the academic rigor and innovation of the CBC with a deep commitment to Christian discipleship, cultivating learners who are not only capable but Christlike.

Since our founding in 2010, we've grown from a small community school to a leading Christian educational institution, serving over 300 students annually.
